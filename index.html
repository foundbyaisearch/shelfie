How it works

Three steps. No developer needed.

You install it like any other Shopify app. Shelfie does the technical part so you do not have to touch a single line of code.

Connect your store

One click from the Shopify App Store. Shelfie reads your product catalog automatically. Setup takes about two minutes.

See where you stand
